Local Slang

Zeroed —
Being 'zeroed' means your digital ID is flagged and effectively erased from public registries. People who get zeroed lose access to city services and may be hunted by enforcement nets.
Patch —
Quick, often shady firmware fix. Useful but unstable.
Bloom —
When neon spills across the skyline after a rain; also used to describe viral art.

Tips — How to not get zeroed

  1. Keep a burner identity for public-facing interactions. Never use primary creds in bazaars.
  2. Carry analogue cash for small vendors — offline trades can't trace you.
  3. Patch your comms only at trusted booths; avoid pop-up shacks promising instant ID upgrades.
  4. Don't connect to open mesh nodes that ask for your biometric handshake.
  5. If an enforcement drone queries you, present a neutral posture and comply; escalation draws cameras.
